## About Me
I am Ryusei Miura, a first-year Master's student in the Department of Systems and Control Engineering at Institute of Science Tokyo. 

Currently, I am a member of the [Nakadai Lab](https://www.ra.sc.e.titech.ac.jp/), researching speech enhancement (SE) and automatic speech recognition (ASR) in ego-noise environments, such as those generated by drones.
